### 💧 DEPRESSIVE

| Checklist Label | Description | Maps To | Notes |
|-----------------|-------------|---------|-------|
| Sad empty hopeless | Down, flat, or hopeless most of the day | **Dep #1 (CORE)** | One of two core criteria — at least one must be present |

## Things to consider

**Overlaps (same criterion, multiple items — only one can count):**
- Manic B6 has 4 items (high-energy, nonstop-activity, restless-agitation, agitated-depressed) — could these be consolidated?
- Manic B7 has 3 items (reckless-choices, bizarre-behavior, aggressive-destructive) — distinct enough?
- Dep #6 has 2 items (no-energy, selective-energy) — are both useful or is "selective energy" redundant?
- Dep #2 has 2 items (lost-interest, withdrawn) — withdrawal is a distinct observable behavior but maps to the same criterion
- Dep #3 has 2 items (eating-more, eating-less) — intentionally split since they're opposite presentations of the same criterion

**Sparse coverage:**
- B1, B3, B4, B5 each have only 1 item — are there additional observable behaviors that could map to these?

**Unmapped items:**
- 3 items (denies-anything-wrong, mood-swings, unusual-anxiety) don't count toward any criterion — keep as observational or drop?
